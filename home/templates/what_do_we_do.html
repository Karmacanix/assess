The Assessment Guide


Have you found a mobile or cloud application that would really help your team out?

Have you wondered, is it safe to use? Is it secure? Is it private? That's what this service is all about. We have assembled a short list of questions from our exp[erts in ICT and the Privacy team to help you evaluate the risk. In the end it's up to you and your team but if it's too risky for the privacy and safety of our patient data we'll let you know. And can probably help you find an alternative. Register your application answer a few questions and we'll give you the stamp of approval to go ahead and get your time saving, life saving app.
